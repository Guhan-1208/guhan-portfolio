Fortnite

Fortnite is an online video game and game platform developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in six distinct game mode versions: Fortnite Battle Royale, Save the World, Creative, Lego Fortnite, Rocket Racing, and Fortnite Festival. Battle Royale especially became a cultural phenomenon, drawing over 125 million players in its first year and generating billions in revenue.
