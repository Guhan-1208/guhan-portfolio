League of Legends

League of Legends is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Riot Games. Players control champions with unique abilities, with over 160 champions as of 2023. The objective is to destroy the enemy’s nexus. LoL has become the world’s biggest esport with millions of players and viewers globally. Riot has expanded the universe with spin-offs, music, comics, and the hit animated series *Arcane*.
