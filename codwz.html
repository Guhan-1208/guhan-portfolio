Call Of Duty - Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale game developed by Raven Software and Infinity Ward. Released on March 10, 2020, it featured 150-player matches, cross-platform play, and iconic gameplay modes like Battle Royale and Plunder. Players collected in-game currency, bought Loadout Drops, and used custom classes with killstreaks, gas masks, and more. At launch, Warzone supported Trios, with Solos, Duos, and Quads added in later updates. Its intense combat mechanics and strategic gameplay made it a fan favorite in the shooter genre.
